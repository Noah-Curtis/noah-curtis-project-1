LONDON

London is a city steeped in history and culture, and has played a pivotal role in shaping the world. From the Roman Empire to the British Empire, London has been a center of power, commerce, and innovation. Its iconic landmarks, such as Big Ben and Buckingham Palace, symbolize the city's rich heritage. As a global financial hub and a melting pot of cultures, London continues to influence fashion, music, art, and literature. Its vibrant arts scene, world-class museums, and diverse neighborhoods make it a captivating destination for visitors from around the globe.
