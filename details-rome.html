ROME

Rome is an eternal city that stands as a testament to the grandeur of the Roman Empire. Its ancient ruins, including the Colosseum and the Roman Forum, continue to awe visitors. As the birthplace of the Latin language and Roman law, Rome's cultural influence is profound. Its artistic and architectural heritage, from Renaissance masterpieces to Baroque churches, shapes the world's aesthetic sensibilities. Today, Rome remains a captivating city, blending its rich history with modern-day life, attracting millions of tourists each year.
