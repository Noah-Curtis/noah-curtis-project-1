LOS ANGELES

Los Angeles is a sprawling city that is a global center for entertainment, technology, and culture. Its iconic landmarks, such as the Hollywood Sign and the Santa Monica Pier, symbolize its glamorous image. As a diverse city, Los Angeles offers a mix of cultures, from Mexican cuisine to Korean fashion. Its film industry, renowned worldwide, continues to shape popular culture.
